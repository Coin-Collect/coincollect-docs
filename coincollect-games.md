# CoinCollect Games

CoinCollect Games

CoinCollect Games revolutionize Play-to-Earn by merging Web3 rewards with immersive gaming. In partnership with QuestGalaxy (https://questgalaxy.com), CoinCollect brings true digital ownership, cross-chain utility, and community engagement to gamers.

### Why Web3 Gaming Matters

Traditional gaming locks achievements and rewards inside closed platforms. With Web3, players gain real ownership—unlocking NFTs, tradable tokens, and decentralized governance. In CoinCollect Games, your progress turns into real assets and new opportunities.

### CoinCollect: The Rewards Engine

• Stake NFTs in pools to earn tokens. • Claim rewards instantly via the personalized dashboard. • Track assets across multiple chains easily.

### QuestGalaxy: Building Fun + Growth

QuestGalaxy (https://questgalaxy.com) builds Web3-native games with quests, leaderboards, competitions, and NFT-gated challenges. It's also a platform for decentralized communities, using social quest tools and Lens Protocol for portable profiles and NFTs.

### The Partnership: Games Powered by Rewards

• NFT-Gated Games: KEY NFTs and partner NFTs unlock access. • Play-to-Earn: Earn COLLECT tokens, QuestGalaxy tokens, and special NFTs. • Milestone Achievements: Progress generates collectible, tradable checkpoints. • Seasonal Events: Leaderboards, exclusive loot, and big rewards.

### What's In It For Players?

• Immersive gameplay and exclusive NFT access. • Earn COLLECT tokens, QuestGalaxy tokens, limited edition NFTs. • Special access using KEY NFTs and partner NFTs. • Connected multi-chain economy.

### Getting Started

1. Connect your wallet to CoinCollect.
2. Use a KEY NFT (or eligible NFT) to unlock games.
3. Play games and quests in QuestGalaxy.
4. Claim rewards directly on CoinCollect's dashboard.
