# Stake Lens Profile NFT (Lens Protocol Pool)

## Overview

The Lens Protocol Pool lets you **stake Lens Profile NFTs** on CoinCollect and earn rewards when the pool is active.

## Eligibility

- Hold the eligible NFT collection(s) for this pool
- Connect your wallet to CoinCollect

## How to stake a Lens Profile NFT

1. Open the staking page: https://app.coincollect.org/nftpools
2. Connect your wallet
3. Find the **Lens Protocol** pool
4. Select NFTs to stake and confirm the transaction

## Rewards

Reward token(s), APY, and pool duration are shown in the pool UI.
